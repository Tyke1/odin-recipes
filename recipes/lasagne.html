Recipe for Lasagne Alla Bolognese Saporite

This is the classic lasagne alla Bolognese recipe from the Emilia region in Northern Italy.

The Bolognese sauce is made with a mixture of beef and pork mince. The addition of prosciutto, red wine, cinnamon, and nutmeg make it truly authentic.

Ingredients

  • 11 ounces ground beef
  • 21 ounces peeled San Marzano tomatoes
  • 11 ounces lasagna noodles
  • 2 ½ cups grated Parmesan cheese, or to taste

Steps

  1. Mix Beef and tomatoes to make bolognese
  2. Place pasta sheets over bolognese sauce with a layer of cheese between
  3. Bake 220C
